APPLY FOR UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

Pursue a Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Sciences, Social & Management Sciences, or Arts & Humanities at Anchor University Lagos.

APPLY FOR POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMMES

Advance your career with our postgraduate diplomas, Master's, and doctoral programmes designed for working professionals and researchers.

Anchor University Lagos is a private Christian university owned by the Deeper Christian Life Ministry. Established in 2016, the university is committed to raising godly and excellent leaders for national and global impact.

Applicants must possess a minimum of five O'Level credits including English and Mathematics. UTME and Post-UTME screening are required. Direct Entry candidates with relevant qualifications are also welcome to apply.

Anchor University offers merit-based scholarships, bursaries, and flexible payment plans to support students from diverse financial backgrounds. Contact the Bursary for details.

Our campus features modern lecture halls, well-equipped laboratories, a digital library, sports complex, chapel, and comfortable hostel accommodation for students.
